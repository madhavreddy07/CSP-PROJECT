About VDRMS

The Village Drainage and Roads Management System (VDRMS) is an online platform designed to help improve the condition of village infrastructure ‚Äî especially drainage systems and rural roads. It connects villagers, panchayat officials, and engineers through a single system that allows reporting, monitoring, and resolving local problems quickly and efficiently.

How the System Works

  • 1. Report Issues: Villagers can report problems such as potholes, waterlogging, or blocked drains directly through the system.
  • 2. Automatic Prioritization: The system sorts reports based on severity and location for quick response.
  • 3. Engineer Dashboard: Officials and engineers can view issues on a real-time dashboard and assign repair teams.
  • 4. Status Tracking: Villagers can track updates until their issue is resolved.
  • 5. Data Insights: Collected data helps in long-term planning for sustainable infrastructure.

Our Mission

To ensure every village has safe, clean, and accessible roads with a well-maintained drainage system that prevents flooding, improves transportation, and promotes a healthier community.
